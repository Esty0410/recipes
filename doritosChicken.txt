Ingredients:
200 gram flower
50 ml halffull milk
2 chicken
2 eggs
1 bag Doritos Nacho Cheese
peper
How to make it:
pre-heat the oven at 200 degrees Celcius
mix the egg, milk and pepper in a bowl
get a bowl of the flower
and the last bowl you add the cruched Doitos
the you take the chicken through the flower, then the egg/milk mixture and last the Doritos
put for 20 minutes in the oven and enjoy
good luck!
